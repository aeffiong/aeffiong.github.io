About Me

Hi, my name is Atim Effiong, and I am a Utah native who loves fitness and my dog Butters. I have always had an interest in building things but wasn't sure in what capacity. I have found that coding has a been a great outlet for me to create.

I grew up in Ogden and a attended a small school where I played soccer, basketball, and ran track. To this day, I still have 2 state records for track. One in the 100m dash and another one in the 4x100m relay race. I attended the University of Utah and received a Bachelor's in Exercise and Sport Science with minors in both Nutrtion, and Human Development. I then attended the University of Arizona where I received a Master's in Public Health. I moved back to Utah after completely my degree in 2013. Since then I have been a study coordinar where I data analysis for large research study database, started an online business personal training, and currently work in the employee wellness sector.
